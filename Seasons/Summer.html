SUMMER

With its long, bright days and warm temperatures, summer represents the peak of the year's vitality. This season is characterized by the intense heat of the sun, which brings people outdoors to enjoy activities like swimming, picnics, and vacations. The lush greenery of spring continues to thrive during summer, providing a perfect backdrop for leisurely strolls and outdoor adventures. Gardens flourish, filled with vibrant flowers and delicious fruits, while animals revel in the abundance of resources. Summer is a time of joy and relaxation, a period to celebrate life and savor the bounty of nature.

    FACTS

  • Summer is the hottest season of the year with longer days and shorter nights.
  • It occurs between June and August in the Northern Hemisphere and between December and February in the Southern Hemisphere.
  • The word "summer" originates from the Old English word "sumor" which is believed to be related to the Germanic word "sumaraz" meaning "good, beautiful."
